My recommendation: B (Guided Prep Flow) — it gets the highest fraction of candidates through one full loop (gap → mock → study plan), which is where the differentiation lives. A is great for retention but assumes the candidate comes back; C is the most novel but bets the experience on voice quality. We can borrow elements: B's flow with A's "home base" entry card, or B's flow with a small Mira-style intro screen.
